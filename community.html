Why fly and stream flight sim when you fly IRL?

I decided to start streaming when I started my flight training back in 2013. Had a short break when I started flying commercially, but with newer sims such as MSFS being way better and more realistic than the old simulators, I got back into it.

I find it enjoyable to show a very professional and real-life relatable operation in the simulator. At the same time I also like to be able to give something back to the community and possibly teach others who want to walk the same path as I have done. Without flight sims keeping my passion up throughout the years I most likely would not fly airliners IRL today.

It is also a way for myself to continuously repeat procedures, rules and just general knowledge. For instance I did not fly anything but flight sim for 1 year during the Covid pandemic — and yet it felt like I had just flown a few days ago when I got back into it.

If you have any questions about aviation, flying, or what it's like to work as a Commercial Pilot, please feel free to ask. I try to look through the chat when I am not in too busy phases of flight.
