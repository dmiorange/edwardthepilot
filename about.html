The Pilot Behind the Stream

About Me

Commercial pilot, flight sim enthusiast, Twitch streamer.

Hi there, I'm Edward!

I am 31 years old, born and raised in Sweden. I always dreamed of becoming a pilot since I was 6 years old. I started pursuing my dream in 2013 and have since 2016 worked as a commercial pilot.

After working mainly nighttime flying cargo all across Europe for five years on a turboprop and the Bombardier CRJ, I started flying the world's most modern single-aisle airliner, the Airbus A220, in 2022.

I got my first flight sim at the age of six and have continuously used various simulators ever since. Flight sims are a great way to get as close as possible to real aviation without leaving your home. And if you decide to start flying in real life, they're a fantastic tool to practice flows, checklists, and radio phraseology right at your PC.
